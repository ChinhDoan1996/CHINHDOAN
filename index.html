A) Overview

This website name is “PESG”, the sellers can sell a lot of product categories such as food & beverage, clothes, book, electronic good house equipment, healthy and beauty, etc. The product quality will be control by production team to make sure every good will be the best and no complaint about it.

B) Motivation
-The eCommerce Market in Australia was predict worth about USD 12.8 billion and will rise up to USD 18.7 billion by 2020. Online shopping in Australia account 8% of total traditional retailers in 2017. In Australia, people consume $21.3 billion for buying online, 18.7% increase in total. This is a huge potential market to develop a business. With PESG, the online customers can buy the products with cheaper price and more product quality guarantee. Moreover, the seller's can set up their shop without any monthly fees. It will help the seller's can open shop online with lower cost and help them reach more customers.

C) Descriptiom
PESG have many feature to help users have good experience in the website. Users can create the account to shop online, see past order and see online receipt. Moreover, the customers can review the product quality and also see percentage of rating of this product. The website have many categories of product and divide logically, the users can easy access and choose which product they want to buy without confusing. They can easy to access to the social media page such as Facebook, Instagram and Twitter. The search engine will provide the suggestion result relevant with what they inserted. The result ensure that relevant product will show up. The users do not lose in large categories of product and can get the good search result by good search engine. PESG have appears in top ten result base on good content, detail product description and contain many keyword per page and good SEO campaign to reach the top of Google search.The website have good optimizing with all platform (mobile, tablet and website) and less time for loading page ( two minutes). The sellers can analyses the traffic to see how many users come to the website and look into their product by Google Analytics. They can know where the users come from, which product consumers care the most. Next, they can see the percentage of potential customers become the retail customers to know which product is good and which one is need to improve. The website can present to customer which shop is located near customer’s address and estimated the delivery fee. PESG provide one day shipping without extra when the customers spent more than $100AUD. The customers can become the VIP members to have many benefits such as 2 hours shipping, 100% refund if product broken…, it subscribe service only 50$ per month.
